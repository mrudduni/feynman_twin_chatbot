# 🚀 Quick Features Reference

## Three New Features Added ✨

### 1. 🎤 Voice Interaction
**Talk to and hear from Feynman!**

| Button | Function | Status |
|--------|----------|--------|
| 🎤 | Voice Input (Speech-to-Text) | Click to record your question |
| 🔊/🔇 | Voice Output Toggle | Hear responses spoken aloud |

**How to use:**
1. Click 🎤 → Speak your question → Click again to stop
2. Toggle 🔊 to enable/disable voice responses
3. Browser will ask for microphone permission (first time)

---

### 2. 🧠 Memory Dashboard
**See what the AI remembers!**

**Access**: Click "🧠 Memory Dashboard" link or visit http://127.0.0.1:5173/memory.html

**What you'll see:**
- 📊 **Statistics**: Total interactions, insights, topics
- 💬 **Recent Chats**: Last 10 Q&A pairs
- 💡 **Insights**: Key learnings captured
- 🏷️ **Topics**: What you've discussed (with counts)
- ⚙️ **Preferences**: Your saved preferences

**Auto-refresh**: Updates every 10 seconds

---

### 3. 📅 Timeline Awareness
**Feynman knows when he is!**

The digital twin now:
- ✅ Knows current date (June 2, 2026)
- ✅ References Feynman's lifespan (1918-1988)
- ✅ Acknowledges being a digital reconstruction
- ✅ Says "In my time..." when discussing past
- ✅ Expresses curiosity about modern developments
- ✅ Distinguishes timeless vs. era-specific knowledge

**Example response:**
> "Well, in my time (1918-1988), we were just beginning to understand quantum computers. I passed away in 1988, so I can't speak to modern developments. But the fundamental quantum principles I taught are timeless..."

## Quick Start

### Using Voice
```
1. Open chat at http://127.0.0.1:5173
2. Click 🎤 microphone button
3. Speak: "What is quantum entanglement?"
4. Response is spoken aloud automatically
5. Toggle 🔊 to disable voice if needed
```

### Viewing Memory
```
1. Click "🧠 Memory Dashboard" in header
2. See all your interactions and insights
3. Click "🔄 Refresh" to update
4. Return to chat with "← Back to Chat"
```

### Timeline Context
```
Automatic! Just ask questions like:
- "What do you think about modern physics?"
- "Explain quantum mechanics"
- "What was science like in your time?"

Feynman will contextually reference time periods.
```

## Tips & Tricks

### Voice Tips
- 🎤 Speak clearly and at normal pace
- 🔇 Disable voice output in quiet environments
- 🎧 Use headphones to prevent echo
- 🌐 Works best in Chrome/Edge

### Memory Tips
- 📊 Check dashboard to track learning progress
- 💡 More conversations = more insights captured
- 🏷️ Topics auto-categorize your interests
- 🔄 Refreshes automatically every 10 seconds

### Timeline Tips
- 📅 Ask about "modern" topics to see temporal awareness
- 🕐 Feynman will distinguish his era from current time
- 📚 Timeless physics principles explained regardless of era
- 🔮 Shows curiosity about post-1988 developments

## Troubleshooting

### Voice not working?
- Grant microphone permission
- Check browser compatibility
- Refresh the page
- Try different browser

### Memory dashboard empty?
- Have at least one conversation first
- Ensure backend is running (port 8000)
- Click refresh button
- Check browser console

### Timeline not mentioned?
- Try detailed answer length
- Ask about modern/current topics
- Restart backend if needed
